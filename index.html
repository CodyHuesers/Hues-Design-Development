Why Go Custom?

The most important aspect of your website and brand is SEO (Search Engine Optimization). Without proper SEO your website is essentially a cool, yet expensive, business card. A custom-built website allows for full control of the code and can be unique to your brand. Template-style websites, such as Wordpress, do include some SEO tools, but they are limited and can be difficult to use. SEO with a custom website allows for full control--from structure to blog posts to branding images, you name it.

Custom websites are more search-engine friendly. This means having someone like me build your website and handle the SEO can help increase revenue to your business by gaining more customers and having a stronger presence online. You want to grow your business, not just be another website online.

Choosing to go custom makes for a more cohesive look. The design is 100% tailor-made to fit your company and brand image. While template-style website builders can do a decent job, you are using what is likely a layout built years ago that millions of others have also chose. With a custom design your brand's colors, fonts, logos, buttons, navigation, will all match throughout, making for a professional website that is unique to YOU. Going custom isn't just about building a website-- It's about building your brand or company into what you envisioned it to be.
